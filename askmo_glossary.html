> ASKMO GLOSSARY OF MODERN MISUNDERSTOOD TECH TERMS

AI (Artificial Intelligence)
What managers yell when they want to sound cutting edge. Actual understanding not required.
Cloud
Someone else’s computer, now with 10x the cost and 1/10th the control.
Wiki
A documentation graveyard that nobody reads but everyone insists you create.
Agile
A process that promises flexibility but mostly delivers more meetings and less progress.
Digital Transformation
A phrase used to justify a new dashboard. No actual transformation required.
Synergy
Corporate Mad Libs. Filler word for PowerPoints when no one knows what’s happening.
